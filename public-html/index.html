What Is GGT Outdoors?

GGT Outdoors was created by Parker Cotts to share his love for the outdoors and God. The company's motto, God's Got This, came from a dear friend who believes that God has everything under control. We hope you enjoy the stories shared around the fire and begin to love the outdoors like we do.
