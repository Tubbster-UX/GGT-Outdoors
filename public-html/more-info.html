What is GGT Outdoors

GGT Outdoors was created by Parker Cotts so that he could share his love for the outdoors as well as his love for God. The company's motto, God’s Got This, came from a dear friend who believed that God has everything under his control. Parker noticed that God does have everything under control and will be with you wherever you are whether that is deep in the deer woods or on a quiet lake fishing. Parker has expanded and asked a few friends to join him to make his dream crew of outdoor loving God fearing guys to bring the outdoors to you. Whether you are watching our youtube videos, liking our posts, or interacting with our forum, we hope you enjoy the stories shared around the fire and begin to love the outdoors and God the way we do. #godsgotthis
